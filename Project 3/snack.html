Snacks

Maybe you need a little something between meals, thats okay! Follow these recipes if you're looking quick and easy snack to hold you over.

Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad

Chicken Caesar Salad is a must in the recipe file and this one definitely does not disappoint! This salad is perfectly flavorful and deliciously satisfying, everyone will love this!

Turkey Bacon BLT

Made with turkey bacon, whole wheat toast and light mayonnaise, this BLT sandwich is just as tasty as the classic version.

Peanut Butter And Jelly Sandwich

It's just a classic. You can make this sandwhich with organic peanut butter, sugar free jelly, and whole wheat bread to make a simple and nutritious meal.

Pasta Salad

This is a perfect meal to bring to school. Fresh and easy pasta salad packed with crisp vegetables, fresh mozzarella, and tossed with a simple homemade dressing.
